Title: Gentoo-zh overlay for Chinese users
Author: microcai <mirocaicai@gmail.com>
Content-Type: text/plain
Posted: 2011-06-12
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 1.0

On 2011-06-12, gentoo-zh overlay is merged from gentoo-china overlay and
gentoo-taiwan overlay. All users of gentoo-china overlay and gentoo-taiwan
overlay is encouraged to migrate to gentoo-zh overlay, The two overlays will
stop updating eventually,

run: layman -f 
run: layman -d gentoo-china ; layman -d gentoo-taiwan 
run: layman -a gentoo-zh
